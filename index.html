    INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Before continuing with the installation of this product, please find a suitibale area for placement. Dimensions are listed below and can be found on the insert within the packaging.

    2. Children

  2. When considering the placemnt of this item, please ensure that it is not active in an enclosed space where children may be sleeping. Doing so may lead to suffocation or complicate existing health conditions.

  3. It is imperative for the sake of the devcie as well as your child that the device is placed in a location that is not within reach of the child

    4. Electrical Components

  4. This device requires an electric outlet to be used. The plug is polarized, meaning one metal pin is wider than the other. If the plug does not fit within the outlet, try flipping it around.

  5. Beware of damaged outlets as well. Inserting the plug into a damaged outlet can result in power outages, device damage in in other cases, physical damage or death.

  6. In addition, when plugging in the device, do not leave the cable loose, as it may lead to damaged property or injury.

  7. Ensure that when handling any mechanical or elctrical aspect of this device, you do so in a safe environment with dry hands. Failing to do so may result in physical injury or death.

    8. General Placemnt

  8. This device creates a lot of moisture in the surrounding area, so be sure to leave 4 inches of space between the device and the wall

  9. Similarly, placing underneath an overhanging surface will create moisture. To avoid the decay of the surrounding area, it is advised not to place the device under any over hanging surfaces, such as a kitchen cabinet.

  10. This device is made of materials that are sensitive to heat. Do not place near heat sources such as stoves, ovens, microwaves, or fireplaces.

  11. This device is meant for outdoor use only. Placing outdoors will often-times have no effect, or damage the product

  12. Uneven surfaces may result in the device tipping over on falling, potentially breaking

    13. Cleaning

  13. Never use detergents, glass cleaner, furniture polish, paint thinner, or other household solvents to clean any part of the humidifier.

  14. This device operates on water only. Do not attempt to operate the device with any other unauthorized liquids.
